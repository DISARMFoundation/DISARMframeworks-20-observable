# Technique T0180.010: Intersectional Discriminatory Content

**Summary**: Discriminatory Content is defined as harmful or negative messaging that perpetuates stereotypes, biases, or prejudices based on personal characteristics such as sexual orientation, gender, race, religion, disability, or other legally protected social identities or attributes. This content often reinforces harmful societal norms and can contribute to the marginalisation or stigmatisation of certain groups.<br><br>Intersectional Discrimination occurs when content discriminates against two or more protected characteristics.
